Infiltrate. Sabotage. Survive.

First released in 1987, Airborne Ranger represents the pinnacle of tactical action on the Commodore 64. As an elite ranger, you are dropped behind enemy lines to complete high-stakes missions ranging from prisoner rescues to bunker demolitions. Every step requires careful planning, as ammunition and supplies are limited.
